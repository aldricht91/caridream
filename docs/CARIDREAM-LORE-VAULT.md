# CariDream Lore Vault

**Status:** Research Archive  
**Canon authority:** None until explicit promotion  
**Governed by:** [CariDream World Bible Canon Edition v2.7](./CARIDREAM-WORLD-BIBLE.md)
**Initialized:** June 10, 2026

## Purpose

The CariDream Lore Vault preserves folklore discoveries, cultural traditions, legends, creatures, locations, symbols, oral-history notes, and story concepts before they are considered for canon.

An entry in this archive is not automatically true within the Caribbean Dreamworld.

Nothing in the Lore Vault may override the World Bible.

## Archive Rules

1. Research enters the Lore Vault before it enters a CariDream story as established lore.
2. Every entry must identify its island or cultural context.
3. Cultural claims should include reliable sources or consultation notes.
4. Sacred, living, or sensitive traditions require respectful review.
5. The entry must explain how it contributes something unique under the Island Rule.
6. Fear-based material must be adapted toward wonder, reflection, and emotional safety.
7. A lesson may guide development, but the final story must never state the lesson didactically.
8. No entry becomes canon without an explicit promotion decision.
9. Promotion requires a corresponding World Bible update and version-history entry.
10. Conflicting proposals remain research notes and may not alter a Permanent Pillar.

## Entry Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `Researching` | Sources and cultural context are still being gathered. |
| `Needs Cultural Review` | The concept requires sensitivity or authenticity review. |
| `Story Exploration` | The concept is suitable for non-binding story development. |
| `Canon Review` | A complete proposal is being checked against the World Bible. |
| `Promoted to Canon` | Explicitly approved and added to a versioned World Bible. |
| `Deferred` | Preserved for later consideration. |
| `Rejected` | Conflicts with canon, cultural guidance, or CariDream philosophy. |

## Promotion Checklist

Before promotion, confirm:

- The concept has clear Caribbean cultural grounding.
- The island or community is identified accurately.
- Available sources and consultation notes are recorded.
- The concept adds a unique island perspective.
- It does not duplicate an existing guardian, theme, role, or sacred phenomenon.
- It does not contradict the Cycle of Whispers.
- It does not alter Manman Mirwa's role.
- It does not turn the Moonlight Wanderer into the hero.
- It does not give the Moon Conch authority to command.
- It protects Wonder Over Fear.
- It supports Sleep First.
- Its lesson can be discovered naturally.
- Its relationship to existing series and characters is defined.
- Cultural sensitivities and permissions have been considered.
- The exact World Bible change has been drafted.

## Research Lanes

The following lanes are recognized by the World Bible as areas for future research. The lanes are canonical development directions; individual findings within them remain non-canon until promoted.

### LV-001: Sea Spirits

- Status: Researching
- Scope: Island-specific sea traditions, ocean guardians, currents, reefs, fishing knowledge, and maritime wonder.
- Canon guardrail: Must not duplicate the Sea Turtle's role as Keeper of Ocean Stories.
- Island Rule question: What relationship with the sea is unique to the represented island or community?

### LV-002: Volcanic Spirits

- Status: Researching
- Scope: Volcanic landscapes, geothermal features, creation traditions, renewal, warmth, and patient change.
- Canon guardrail: Must remain comforting and must not rely on eruption danger.
- Island Rule question: Which island's volcanic landscape provides the distinct perspective?

### LV-003: Ancient Taíno Lore

- Status: Needs Cultural Review
- Scope: Carefully sourced Taíno history, living Indigenous identity, symbols, language, nature relationships, and cultural memory.
- Canon guardrail: Requires cultural sensitivity review and must not combine distinct traditions into a generic mythology.
- Island Rule question: Which specific community, island, period, or living tradition is represented?

### LV-004: Maroon Wisdom Stories

- Status: Needs Cultural Review
- Scope: Community, resilience, land knowledge, memory, freedom, music, and intergenerational wisdom.
- Canon guardrail: Must not soften, fictionalize, or detach the subject from its historical and cultural context.
- Island Rule question: Which specific Maroon community and tradition informs the story?

### LV-005: Fisherman Legends

- Status: Researching
- Scope: Navigation, weather wisdom, boats, harbors, moonlit journeys, family traditions, and respectful relationships with the sea.
- Canon guardrail: Must distinguish each island's fishing culture and avoid generic coastal imagery.
- Island Rule question: What local practice, landscape, vessel, or oral tradition makes the story specific?

### LV-006: Hummingbird Tales

- Status: Story Exploration
- Scope: Hummingbirds as messengers of wonder, movement, beauty, memory, or rest.
- Related canon: The Doctor Bird is a Tier 6 Island Legend.
- Canon guardrail: New hummingbird characters must not erase the Doctor Bird's Jamaican identity.
- Island Rule question: What species, tradition, landscape, or symbolism belongs to the represented island?

### LV-007: Silk Cotton Traditions

- Status: Needs Cultural Review
- Scope: Silk Cotton trees, community memory, sacred landscapes, folklore, rest, listening, and ancestral presence.
- Related canon: `Whispers of Papa Bois`.
- Canon guardrail: Traditions vary across islands and must not be presented as interchangeable.
- Island Rule question: Which island-specific relationship with the Silk Cotton tree is being preserved?

### LV-008: Caribbean Mermaid Traditions

- Status: Needs Cultural Review
- Scope: Island-specific water beings, mermaid traditions, beauty, transformation, longing, memory, and ocean wonder.
- Canon guardrail: Avoid collapsing distinct figures into a generic mermaid archetype.
- Island Rule question: Which named tradition and cultural context gives the figure its identity?

## Haiti Lore Vault Promotion Record

### Original Proposal

Haiti's flagship mythology began as a Major Series proposal centered on rhythm, harmony, balance, resilience, memory, Métrès Lumi, the Marasa Winds, the Lumi Spirits, the Silver Drum Tide, and the Lantern of Jacmel.

### Canon Review Process

The proposal was reviewed against the Permanent Pillars, the Cycle of Whispers, Manman Mirwa, the Moonlight Wanderer, the Moon Conch, Great Spirits, existing Guardians, existing Sacred Phenomena, current Major Series, the Island Rule, and existing Haiti stories.

### Boundary Review Process

[Haiti Canon Boundaries v1.0](./HAITI-CANON-BOUNDARIES-V1.0.md) established binding authority limits, overlap protections, cultural guardrails, protected uncertainties, and long-term franchise boundaries. The Golden Rhythm's interim Foundational Force classification was later reviewed against its protected-mystery requirements.

### Canon Promotion Approval

World Bible v2.6 completes the promotion by classifying the Golden Rhythm as a Foundational Mystery, preserving Métrès Lumi as a Great Mystery, retaining the Marasa Winds as Regional Guardians, retaining the Lumi Spirits as Regional Spirits, retaining the Silver Drum Tide as a Sacred Phenomenon, retaining the Lantern of Jacmel as a Foundational Legend, and confirming `Whispers of the Golden Rhythm` as an Official Canon Series.

The Golden Rhythm remains unexplained and unowned. Haiti remembers it more often than elsewhere, but does not define it for the wider Caribbean Dreamworld.

## Season 1 Promotion Record

The official [Whispers of the Golden Rhythm Season 1 Bible](./WHISPERS-OF-THE-GOLDEN-RHYTHM-SEASON-1-BIBLE.md) establishes ten episodes:

1. `The Twin Breezes`
2. `The Lantern Path`
3. `The Humming Shore`
4. `The Mountain That Remembers`
5. `The Driftwood Drum`
6. `The Lantern of Jacmel`
7. `The Moonlit Steps`
8. `The Rhythm Beneath the Reef`
9. `The Night of Two Winds`
10. `The Golden Drum Tide`

The season structure introduces the Marasa Winds, presents the first Lumi Spirit appearances, develops the Wanderer's understanding through listening, and gradually increases awareness of the Golden Rhythm without defining it.

Episode 10's production title does not create a new Sacred Phenomenon. Its canonical event is the Silver Drum Tide. The use is approved for Season 1 but does not create a recurring finale requirement.

Full scripts remain subject to episode-level cultural research, continuity review, and sleep-first approval.

**Cultural review record:** The Marasa Winds are promoted only as an original CariDream Dreamworld interpretation. Internal cultural-boundary review was completed; no external consultation is claimed. Any future story drawing directly from living, sacred, or historically documented Haitian Marasa traditions requires specific sourcing and consultation before publication.
