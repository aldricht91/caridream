## New Classifications

### Haiti-Bound Foundational Force

A regional force that establishes Haiti's Dreamworld identity without governing the Cycle, Great Spirits, universal functions, or other islands.

The Golden Rhythm is the first and only current entry.

### Regional Spirits

Local presences that reveal an island or region's established identity without embodying universal forces or maintaining Guardian authority.

The Lumi Spirits are the first current entry.
