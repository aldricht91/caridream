# CariDream Story Identity Map

## Canon Edition v2.4

**Status:** Story governance companion  
**Library reviewed:** 43 narrated stories  
**Purpose:** Protect each island's distinct voice while identifying theme, lesson, canon relationships, series fit, overlap, and revision priority.

This map does not promote new lore into canon. A Guardian is named only when an established Canon Edition v2.4 Guardian is present or clearly relevant. A Spirit entry describes affinity with an established Great Spirit; it does not mean that Spirit literally appears in the current story.

## Revision Priority

| Priority | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **P1 - High** | Resolve before the library is treated as canon-polished. The story has an overly explicit lesson, substantial thematic duplication, unclear canon role, cultural-review need, or weak series placement. |
| **P2 - Medium** | Preserve the story, but sharpen its island identity, distinguish it from close neighbors, or clarify its place within a series. |
| **P3 - Low** | The identity is already distinct. Only light continuity or prose polish is recommended. |

## Island Identity Boundaries

| Island | Distinct CariDream Voice |
| --- | --- |
| **Aruba** | Trade-wind direction, desert resilience, Divi-Divi guidance, Shoco watchfulness, open horizons, and connection across distance. |
| **Bahamas** | Ocean belonging, reef wonder, turtle memory, Exuma light, Eleuthera's pink shore, and the intimate breathing rhythm of sea and sand. |
| **Dominican Republic** | Mountain valleys, rivers, mist, cultivated landscapes, roads, rain, and rest discovered through movement across Quisqueya. |
| **Haiti** | Freedom, ancestral memory, artistic imagination, dignity, endurance, Jacmel creativity, and history carried forward through light and rhythm. |
| **Jamaica** | Anansi wit, communal joy, Doctor Bird grace, Blue Mountain stillness, coastal work, and wisdom expressed through lively character and landscape. |
| **Saint Lucia** | The monumental calm of the Pitons, Soufriere's moonlit bay, Castries harbor memory, rainforest music, bamboo rhythm, and volcanic-island presence. |
| **Trinidad & Tobago** | Papa Bois's forest wisdom, Silk Cotton tradition, living rainforest relationships, gentle observation, and guidance discovered through listening. |

The boundaries above protect both Island Rules: no island should duplicate another island's central identity, and no island should overshadow the voices of the others.

## Canon Safeguards

1. **Guardians are not decorative labels.** Papa Bois, the Sea Turtle, Divi-Divi Spirits, La Diablesse, Cigua Spirits, and Silver Ribbon Spirits retain their defined domains.
2. **Great Spirits are affinities unless written into the story.** A story about water does not automatically contain the Water Spirit.
3. **Island Legends are not automatically Guardians.** The Doctor Bird remains an Island Legend unless formally promoted through canon governance.
4. **Landscapes may feel protective without becoming undeclared Guardians.** This applies especially to mountains, trees, lighthouses, and harbors.
5. **Lessons should remain discovered.** P1 stories should remove explicit moral explanation before adding more plot.
6. **Shared bedtime structures are acceptable; shared island identities are not.** Moonlight, stars, rest, waves, and mist can recur, but their cultural and geographic meaning must change from island to island.
7. **Cultural interpretation enters the Lore Vault first.** Any future use of Indigenous, African-derived, Maroon, spiritual, or living folklore traditions requires research and review before canon promotion.
